Classification of K-indices are as follows:
K0=Inactive
K1=Very quiet
K2=Quiet
K3=Unsettled
K4=Active
K5=Minor storm
K6=Major storm
K7=Severe storm
K8=Very severe storm
K9=Extremely severe storm
As with the K-index, the higher the A-index, the more unstable propagation 
becomes.
Classification of A-indices are as follows:
A0 - A7 = quiet 
A8 - A15 = unsettled
A16 - A29 = active 
A30 - A49 = minor storm 
A50 - A99 = major storm 
A100 - A400 = severe storm
