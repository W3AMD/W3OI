Mark Miller, AK3M (old call WA3KIF)
Mark (AK3M) has been a member of the Lehigh Valley Amateur Radio Club since the late 60's and was first licensed as a Novice in 1969. He enjoys participating in public service activities related to amateur radio (RACES/ARES). He also enjoys chasing DX on HF and is pursuing a personal DXCC. His HF equipment includes a Kenwood TS-430S transceiver, a Van Gordon "Shortie" dipole and MFJ vertical tuned by an MFJ-941E antenna tuner.

Mark is currently on the Board of Governors and, with scant input from LVARC members, creates the E-VOX, the LVARC monthly newsletter.

Mark lives in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania with his wife. He has two children, one son at Moravian College and one daughter who teaches high school English.
