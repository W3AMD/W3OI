Palmerton Rod & Gun Club Inc, Palmerton, PA

Directions


We are located at 225 Gun Club Road, Palmerton PA 18071 in Carbon County, Northeast Pennsylvania.


Coming on Route 209 from the West, we are 4 miles East of Interstate 476 Exit 74 (Mahoning Valley).

Our Club sign is posted at the junction of Route 209 and Stagecoach Road. Turn there , and then turn right on Hazelwood Rd. Go to the 2nd road on left, Gun Club Rd, up the hill on left (about 1/8 mile). The club building is located at the top of the hill ( as marked on the map ).

Coming on Route 209 from the East, we are 2 miles West of Country Junction.

Turn left on Hazelwood Rd next to Jerry's Small Engine. Continue to the split and keep right on Hazelwood Rd. Go to the 2nd road on left, Gun Club Rd, up the hill on left (about 1/8 mile). The club building is located at the top of the hill ( as marked on the map ).

If you are coming from Forest Inn Rd, go up Gun Club Rd to the top of the hill.
