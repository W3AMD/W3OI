W3OI & EPA Dstar Data

The W3OI Dstar repeaters are now on 147.165 (plus offset of 600 Khz) and 445.025 (minus offset of 5 Mhz). If you want to register for the Gateway or need information on setting up the radio please visit the EPA Dstar page as your first step. Also, if you have Dstar capabilities, please join us for a Mid-Atlantic regional net the second and fourth Tuesdays of each month on the 147.165 port with a number of other repeaters in Eastern Pennsylvania, New Jersey and New York City area linked in with W3OI.
