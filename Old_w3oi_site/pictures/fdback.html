Field Day rules include operating for a 24-hour period which includes set up. Stations may not be powered by a commercial electrical source.  All electricity for running equipment, lights, etc. must be supplied by generators, batteries and/or solar power thus simulating emergency operating conditions. Antennas used must be of a portable nature. That is, they must be erected during the set up period.  Permanent antennas are not allowed.   Stations may be contacted by voice, packet radio or Morse code (CW).  Modes used may include FM, single-sideband (SSB), and CW. The Lehigh Valley Amateur Radio Club (W3OI) operates Field Day each year with most of the equipment being supplied by its members with some help from the Lehigh County Emergency Management Agency.
