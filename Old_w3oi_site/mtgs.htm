FIELD DAY!!! June 28 and 29th, 2003

We'll be at Last Year's Location at
Camp Serranova.
Arrive anytime after 5:30 PM Friday the 27st
Upon your arrival, check-in with camp staff in the trailer.
Latitude  40° 28' 15.6'' North (40.471)
Longitude 75º 35' 49.2'' West (-75.597)

Directions:
Take either Rt 29 or Rt 100 south to the stoplight just past the top of Shimerville hill.
Turn right (west) onto St. Peters Road.
Proceed about 4.2 miles on St Peters road.
Be careful or you'll be meeting St. Peter in person!
This road has some wild turns and wild game.
Don't become a statistic on your way to field day...
 Look for camp entrance road on your left. The Serranova signs will be on both sides of the road.
Proceed south on camp road about 0.2 miles and stop to check in at trailer.
Our site will be straight ahead in white building.
Bring your own food, toliet items and sleeping gear.
Camp has kitchen with microwave.
Sleeping area has plywood platform bunks

LVARC July 1st, 2003 Meeting

Fourth in our 2003 Presentation Series:

This month's topic is:

The SuperGainer Receiver!


The SuperGainer Receiver is a combined superhet and superregen design based on "hollow state" components. Should be a treat for anyone who remembers that real radios glow in the dark.

Henry Mohr (W3NCX) will be the guest speaker/presenter. Henry is a charter and life member of the LVARC and keeps abreast of LVARC activities. It'll be a treat just to have him at the meeting. Mark your calendar and stop on by...

Presenter: "Hank" Mohr, W3NCX
